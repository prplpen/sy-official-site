Saving You

by Meli Apt

Summary

"A mysterious new student is sent over to a private academy for one important reason;
to keep tabs on a certain group of girls. Loyal to the one who sent her, she is reluctant
get close to them. But to these girls, they see a potential new friend. Deprived of a normal life, she slowly starts to live the life she secretly longed for: attending school, building friendships, and love...
But will this bliss last forever?"
